I am a master's student and software developer based in Tübingen, Germany.

My main interests are in

  • the computational implementation of logical formalisms, in particular the tableau calculus and modal logic,
  • automation of routine processes, with a focus on administrative tasks in academic education, and
  • workflow improvements in desktop Linux, my specialty being window management and global search in the KDE Plasma desktop environment.

I have a passion and 12 semesters of experience in teaching logic, linguistics and programming.

I am available for contract work. If you are interested in my software development or tutoring, don't hesitate to get in touch.
